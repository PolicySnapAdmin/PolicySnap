PolicySnap Privacy Policy
Effective Date: June 12, 2026
PolicySnap ("we", "our", or "the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that helps users understand insurance policy documents using AI.

1. Information We Collect
Local Storage Only:

Your pasted insurance text and AI summaries (stored locally on your device)
Your usage count for the free tier
Your optional OpenAI API key (stored locally if you choose to enter one)

We do NOT collect or store:

Any personal information
Any insurance policy data on our servers
Any browsing history
Any identifiable user data

2. How We Use Your Data

All processing happens locally on your device or directly through your OpenAI API key.
Your pasted text is sent only to OpenAI’s API when you click “What does this mean?” (if using the free tier) or using your own API key.
No data is sent to our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

    OpenAI: When using the free tier, your text is sent to OpenAI to generate summaries. OpenAI’s privacy policy applies: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy/ 

    Lemon Squeezy: Used only for processing upgrade payments.

4. Data Retention
All data is stored only on your local device using Chrome’s local storage. You can clear it anytime by clearing your browser data or removing the extension.
5. Your Rights
You can:

Delete all saved data by clearing Chrome’s local storage
Remove the extension at any time
Use your own OpenAI API key for full privacy control

6. Children’s Privacy
PolicySnap is not intended for children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify users via the extension or store listing.
